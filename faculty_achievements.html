Faculty Achievements

Dr.S.Vigneshwari, Associate Professor & Head, Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Sathyabama institute of Science & Technology, Chennai, was awarded as Best Young Researcher Computer Science Engineering – Ontology by the Integrated Intelligent Research (IIR) – Republic day Achievers Award on 18th Jan 2018. 

Dr.S.Vigneshwari, Associate Professor & Head, Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Sathyabama institute of Science & Technology, Chennai, was awarded with the title “Innovative Researcher & Dedicated Teaching Professional (Computer Science & Engineering)” on the International Convention on Innovative Scientific Research Strategies (ICISRS-2018) held at Universiti Teknologi MARA, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia on October, 21, 2018.

Dr.B.Bharathi was the keynote speaker in the International Conference GCMT 2018, held in Malaysia. She also was the plenary speaker in the International Conference at Alagappa University, Karaikudi. 

Mr. R. Sethuraman, Assistant Professor, Department of Computer Science and Engineering received a Special memento and appreciation for Concept Note on "Data scientist Role in Digital India" from Dr K R Murali Mohan Scientist-G & Head - DST, Ministry of Science & Technology @ SYPOG-Young Scientist's Conclave-3rd India International Science Festival 2017 held during 13.10.2017 to 16.10.2017.

Divya.S, R. Vignesh, received “Noble Researcher” from International Journal of Research under Literal Access (IJRULA), 2018.
